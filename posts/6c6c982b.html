openssl 检测证书

发表于2023-08-04|更新于2023-08-04
|阅读量:

openssl s_client -connect 127.0.0.1:443 </dev/null | sed -ne '/-BEGIN CERTIFICATE-/,/-END CERTIFICATE-/p' > /tmp/cert.crt && openssl x509 -noout -text -in /tmp/cert.crt && rm /tmp/cert.crt
